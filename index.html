PING - PONG









Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. Stay about 3-4 feet away from your laptop.
  3. Move your right wrist upwards and then downwards to check to see if a red dot appears on the screen where your wrist is.
  4. Press play and distance you self 3-4 feet away.
  5. The red paddle is yours. It will move according to the movement of your wrist.
